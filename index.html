01 — About

Who I Am

I am a Mechanical Engineer from Dhaka, Bangladesh, now specializing in AI-driven robotics in Germany. My background spans classical engineering — fluid mechanics, thermodynamics, control systems — and I am now building deep expertise in machine learning, state estimation, and autonomous robot systems.

Before my Master's, I worked as a Graduate Trainee Engineer at Atlas Copco Bangladesh, conducting energy analysis and commissioning industrial pneumatic systems. I was also Vice President of the AUST Robotics Club, leading 70 members and organizing a national competition with 400+ participants.

My goal is to contribute to research in legged robotics and AI for physical systems — the kind of work being done at ETH Zürich's RSL lab, NVIDIA Robotics, and similar groups pushing the boundary of what autonomous robots can do in the real world.
